PIZZA Recipe

3 Cheese Pizza

Delecious bread, flavorful sauce, decadent 3 cheeses.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Marinara sauce
  • Mozarella Cheese
  • Fontina Cheese
  • Provolone Cheese

Steps

  1. Mix flour, yeast, water into a dough
  2. Allow dough to rise for 30 minutes
  3. Flatten dough into desired pizza shape
  4. Heat oven to 450 degrees.
  5. Add sauce, and 3 shredded cheeses to dough
  6. Cook pizza in the oven until golden brown
  7. Slice and serve
