Bistec Tacos

These melt in your mouth bistec tacos will satisfy your lunch craving. Top with onions and cilantro for extra flavor layers.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Flank steak
  • Cilantro
  • Onions
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Add oil to pan and sear cut flank steak until cooked
  2. Add salt and pepper to meat to taste. Add any spices you like.
  3. Chop white onion and cilantro for the taco toppings.
  4. Heat pan on medium to high, and cook corn tortillas
  5. Fill cooked tortillas with meat
  6. Top with cilantro and onion, enjoy!
